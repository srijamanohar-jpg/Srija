
# TG Social Studies (Class 5 - 7) 📚

This repository contains structured notes and study materials for Social Studies based on the Telangana State Syllabus.

## 📂 Syllabus Overview

### [Class 5]
- **The Earth:** Our home in the Solar System.
- **Continents and Oceans:** Understanding the world map.
- **Early Life:** From gathering to growing food.

### [Class 6]
- **Reading and Making Maps:** Symbols, directions, and scales.
- **The Harappan Civilization:** Ancient urban planning.
- **Agriculture:** How crops are grown and marketed.

### [Class 7]
- **The Delhi Sultanate:** Rise of the Sultanate in India.
- **The Mughal Empire:** Major rulers and administration.
- **Environment:** Natural vegetation and wildlife.
